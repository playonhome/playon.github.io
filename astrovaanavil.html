Astro Vaanavil targets the Indian population and broadcasts in Tamil and various other South Indian dialects. It is the first Malaysian Indian channel to provide top of the range Tamil programmes, Telugu and Malayalam blockbuster movies, top-rated drama series and a variety of general entertainment programmes. Astro Vaanavil is an entertainment, infotainment and a variety channel that provides programming for the whole family. The wide array of content consists of various local broadcasts. Vizhuthugal (Morning Talk show), 360 degree (happenings in town and also a news platform for local celebrities), and many more talent searching programs that target the Indian audience segments also acts as a platform for locals to showcase their abilities.
